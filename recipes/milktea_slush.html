Milk Tea Slush

Description

A delicious and refreshing delight. Introducing the milk tea slushy (pearls not included) by Ice Delights!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp of tea
  • 5 cups of water
  • 2.5 tbsp of evaporated milk
  • 2.5 tbsp of sugar
  • 310 g (around 12 ice cubes) of sugar

Steps

  1. Boil 3 tbsp of tea in 5 cups of water
  2. Put 12 ice cubes inside blender
  3. Measure 2/3 cup of tea then put in blender
  4. Measure 2.5 tbsp of evaporated milk then put in blender
  5. Measure 2.5 tbsp of sugar then put in blender
  6. Put cover on and blend until nice consistency is reached
  7. Serve and Enjoy!
